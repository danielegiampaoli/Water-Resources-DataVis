Water abstraction by source

Water can be removed either permanently or temporarily. Water from mines, drainage and precipitations is included, whereas water used for hydroelectricity generation (in situ use) is excluded.

Top 10 countries can be removed from the plot for better visualisation. Top 10 countries are Greece, France, Germany, Netherlands, Poland, Serbia, Spain, Turkey and United Kingdom.

FSW: Fresh surface water (million m³)
FGW: Fresh groundwater (million m³)
FRW: Combined fresh surface and groundwater (million m³)
