Near surface temperature deviation

The evolution of deviations in average near surface temperature (°C degrees) for Europe, compared to the 1850-1899 average, is shown.
The indicator is part of the EU Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) indicator set and is provided by three different agencies, GISTEMP, HadCRUT and NOAA.
